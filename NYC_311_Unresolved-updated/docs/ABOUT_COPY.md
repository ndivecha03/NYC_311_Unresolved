# About Page — Copy (Verified 2024 Data)

## Intro (combined A + B, expanded)

> Every dark streetlight in New York is a quiet safety gap — and in 2024 alone, more than **30,000 outages** were reported to 311 across the five boroughs. Citywide, nearly **one in four complaints** still sits unresolved a month after it's filed; in Manhattan, the rate climbs to **one in three**. We built this platform to close that loop. Residents report an outage in under 60 seconds, our system classifies the fault, prioritizes it by urgency, and geo-matches it to a vetted local licensed electrician — who receives an AI-drafted, municipally-formatted work order already aligned with NYC DOT standards, ready for same-day approval. The mission is twofold: keep the city that never sleeps lit, and put an estimated **$6–$15M in annual repair revenue** back into the hands of the neighborhood small businesses best positioned to do the work. Faster fixes, stronger local economies, a brighter city — one block at a time.
